8. Diccionario

Diccionario

In this project, it is important to understand and use mathematical vocabulary correctly in English. Below you can find a glossary of key terms that will help you complete the activities and explain your results clearly.

Key vocabulary
Equation
A mathematical statement that shows that two expressions are equal.
👉 Example: 2x + 3 = 7

System of equations
A set of two or more equations that are solved together.

Solution
The value (or values) that make an equation true.

Variable
A letter (usually x or y) that represents an unknown value.

 Inequality
A mathematical expression that shows that two values are not equal.
👉 Symbols: >, <, ≥, ≤

Solve
To find the value of the variable.

Substitution method
A method to solve systems by replacing one variable with an expression.

Elimination method
A method to solve systems by adding or subtracting equations.

Graphical method
A method to solve systems by drawing the equations and finding the intersection point.

Power of 10
A number written as 10 raised to an exponent.
👉 Example: 10² = 100

Infographic
A visual representation of information using images, text and data.

Presentation
Explaining your work to others using clear language and structure.
